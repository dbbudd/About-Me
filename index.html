About me

Daniel Budd
Apple Distinguished Educator · Apple Professional Learning Specialist
School-Wide Technology Team Leader, Hong Kong International School

I'm an Apple Distinguished Educator and Apple Professional Learning Specialist from Perth, Western Australia. At Hong Kong International School, I led the design and development of an inclusive, modern Computer Science program by integrating it into the NCAS Media Arts standards.

I'm a strong advocate for the pedagogical integration of technology, with a particular focus on STEM education, robotics, and the evolving role of AI in shaping teaching and learning. As a speaker, author, and international educator, I've written extensively on coding, app development, and the impact of emerging technology on education — and delivered keynotes and workshops across Australia, South-East Asia, China, and the USA.

Educator, Speaker, Author, Researcher & Learning Designer. Writer of Swift. Lover of design.
