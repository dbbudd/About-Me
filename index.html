About the app

Barriers helps teachers plan better lessons, run them with a built-in classroom timer, and reflect on what worked — all in one document. It's grounded in CAST's Universal Design for Learning (UDL) Guidelines v3.0, and its AI features run entirely on your device.

Free. Private. No accounts, no subscriptions, and nothing is ever sent to a server.
